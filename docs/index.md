<section class="rs-contribute" aria-labelledby="contribute-title">
  <div>
    <p class="rs-kicker">Collection management</p>
    <h2 id="contribute-title">How to add documents</h2>
  </div>
  <ol>
    <li><strong>Add the PDF.</strong> Place the authorised document in <code>docs/assets/pdf/</code>.</li>
    <li><strong>Create its record.</strong> Copy an example page and update its metadata, abstract and citation.</li>
    <li><strong>Connect the collection.</strong> Add the record to its category, navigation and recent additions.</li>
  </ol>
</section>
