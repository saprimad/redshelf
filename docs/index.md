## How to add documents

<div class="rs-steps">
  <div class="rs-step">
    <strong>Add the PDF</strong>
    <p>Place the document in <code>docs/assets/pdf/</code> with a clear, stable filename.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rs-step">
    <strong>Create its page</strong>
    <p>Copy an example document page and update its metadata, abstract and citation.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="rs-step">
    <strong>Add navigation</strong>
    <p>Link the page from its category and, when appropriate, the recently added list.</p>
  </div>
</div>
